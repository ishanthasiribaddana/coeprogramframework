## Step 2: Understanding Program Sections

Each center has **three program sections**:

### 1. Advanced Programs
- Employment-ready skills not currently offered in the curriculum
- Core programs specific to your center

### 2. STEAM Programs  
- Cross-center integration with STEAM Hub
- Collaborative projects combining multiple disciplines

### 3. Cross Center Programs
- Programs requested from or shared with other centers
- Requires selecting the partnering center

---

## Step 3: Adding a New Program

1. Click the **"Add Program"** button in the desired section
2. A new program card will appear
3. Fill in the following fields:

| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Project/Module Name** | Name of the program | "Creative Writing Workshop" |
| **Duration (Hours)** | Estimated hours per term | "20-30" |
| **External Partnerships** | Industry partners involved | "ABC Company, XYZ Foundation" |
| **Career Guidance/Placement** | Companies for career guidance | "Tech Corp, StartUp Inc" |
| **Student Associations** | Click to select relevant associations | Multiple selection allowed |

### For Cross Center Programs Only:
- Select the **Cross Center** dropdown to choose the partnering center

## Step 6: Generating Reports

1. After adding all your programs, click **"Generate Report"** button
2. Review the formatted report
3. Click **"Download Report"** to print or save as PDF
